Welcome to the Climate Change Hub

About This Site

This site is being created by Ryan Smith and Ryan York for HackRice 2020. Our mission with this website is to help you understand what climate change is and how it impact people throughout the world.

Why is this important?

All around the world, we are seeing unprecendented changes brought on by the consumption of fossil fuels. While fossil fuels have fueled the growth of the world, we and many others believe that humans can do better.
